things i'm working on!

colors!

we never really consider colors, or when we do its usually less about the color itself and more about what that color signifies: how gray or green tinged chicken breasts shouldn't be consumed or how the purple spot on skin suggests one was in a physical altercation. In other words, we tend to take colors for granted. (of course, by we, i mean the general population: most of whom are not designers, painters, photographers and such who work and think with colors everyday.) query: can you remember the last time you thought about colors?
through this project i hope to put color in the forefront of our minds. i have created an interactive page where visitors can create things with varying shades of color as their building blocks. my goal is to make people conscious of color and the way colors interact with our environments. i was in part inspired by Robert Hass' poem The Problem of Describing Color.

this project is still in the works, but you can follow along with my progess by clicking this link!
