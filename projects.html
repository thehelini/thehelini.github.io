things i'm working on!

colors!

most people don't really think about colors, or when they do its normally "do these colors match?" or "does this color look good?" most of the times we just take colors for granted. i mean when was the last time you spent more than one minute thinking about colors? through this project i hope for people to see colors in a different way than they normally would. i have created an interactive webpage for my project where users can build their own Mondrian inspired "paintings." the goal is to make people more conscious of color and the way colors interact with their environments, including but not limited to other colors as well as the people viewing them. i was in part inspired by Robert Haas' poem The Problem of Describing Color.

this project is still in the very early stages of making, but you can follow along with my progess by clicking this link!
