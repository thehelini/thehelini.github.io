my name is helena but people call me zucchini!

i study math and philosophy at the University of California Berkeley.

why math? infinites, paradoxes, logic, proofs??! math is fucking beautiful. but perhaps why philosophy is the better question. for one, philosophy isn't just old white men sitting around a table throwing abstract, nonsensical ideas into the air. philosophy, by nature, demands reflection. it forces us to think about the beliefs we usually take for granted; it shows us how we are organized, how our life, our worlds fall into place around those beliefs; it is a map to the muddled and often chaotic landscape of our worlds. we are lost and philosophy is how we find our way around.
